Introduction

Building at the intersection of curiosity and code.

Originally from India, currently based in Italy — constantly learning, building, and exploring what technology can become next.

I'm a young technology enthusiast passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Python, Web Development, Computer Science, and building digital products that matter. Moving from India to Italy reshaped how I see the world — it taught me that curiosity has no borders, and that the best ideas come from paying attention wherever you are.

I believe technology has the power to improve lives and solve meaningful problems. That belief is what pulls me toward AI and Machine Learning — not as buzzwords, but as tools to build things that genuinely help people. Outside of writing code, I enjoy experimenting with new ideas, creating useful websites, and sharing what I learn through my personal blog.

My long-term goal is simple to state and hard to earn: become a successful AI Engineer, entrepreneur, and startup founder — building products that impact millions of people.
